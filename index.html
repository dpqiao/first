Biography

Dapeng Qiao received the Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering of the University of Hong Kong in 2012. He joined the Beijing Institute of Technology in 2013, and is an assistant professor in the school of automation. His research interests include sensor network, MEMS, NFC(acoustic communication), evolutionary algorithm, optimal control and inertial system (accurate turntable).
